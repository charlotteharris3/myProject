1. Musicals

  • Hamilton: The Musical: Lin Manuel Miranda wrote this entire musical on his own after reading a book about the life of Alexander Hamilton.
  • In the Heights: The Musical: Lin Manuel Miranda composed this musical and released it in 2005. He starred as a lead, "Usnavi" and again as an extra in a movie.

2. Movies

  • Mufasa: The Lion King: Lin Manuel Miranda worked on this movie score which propelled the film's attention and revenue.
  • Moana: Miranda played a key role in the creation of this movie, as he helped compose several hit songs such as, "How Far I'll Go"
  • Encanto: his film's notable rap-sing songs were all written with the help of Miranda. This film went on to bring in around $261 Million worldwide.

3. Acting

  • Mary Poppins Returns: Lin Manuel Miranda played a main role in this film as "Jack". He acted, sang, and helped compose the score.
  • Hamilton: The Musical: Lin Manuel Miranda acted in this play as the main character, Alexander Hamilton for approximately 2 years and 3 months.
